Ingredients you would be needing


For the sauce:

  • half pound ground mutton
  • half pound italian sausage
  • one onion
  • two cloves of garlic
  • pasta sauce
  • tomate paste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning

For the lasagnaa

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups of mozarella
  • half cup of parmesian

Steps to your lovely lasagna!


  1. Preheat the oven and then boil the lasagna. Once cooked, drain the water and keep it on the side.
  2. In a large skillet or dutch oven, brown beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium-high heat until no pink remains. Drain any fat.
