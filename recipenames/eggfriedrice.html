Egg fried ricee!





Do you come home hungry after college and then don't have the energy to cook? Do you have leftover rice in your fridge but don't know what to do with it? Well we seem to have the solution. The perfect egg fried rice for students.



The lovely ingredients!


  • Leftover rice
  • an egg
  • onion and salt
  • soy sauce and veggies (only if you have em or skip it)


How to make it?

  1. Add some oil to the pan and fry the onions until golden-brown
  2. Once the onions are done, add the egg and fry it properly
  3. Then add the rice and go round and round until everything is mixed
  4. Add some salt and of course, soya sauce and then mix everything properly
  5. Tada!! It just took you 10 minutes and your dinner is ready!!
