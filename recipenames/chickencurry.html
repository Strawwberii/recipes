Easy Chicken Curry

Do you want to eat the amazing chicken curry that your mom used to make back home? You tried and failed and it tasted dogsh*t? Well, we can help. No no no! Ours won't really taste like your mom"s, her one is God tier but well maybe if you learn this recipe, your tastebuds would definitely love you!

Ingredientss!

  • half kg chicken (like duh!
  • chicken curry masala and turmeric
  • onions
  • ginger and garlic paste
  • soy sauce, yoghurt (option tbh,only for days when you feel fancy)

The legendary steps!

  1. As usual, fry the onions until golden brown
  2. Add the ginger and garlic paste and cook them on medium heat for some time
  3. Add the chicken and cook them for a bit until adding the masala and turmeric
  4. Keep cooking them for long time until you finally smell that curry smell (you know what i mean), then add water and close the lid.
  5. Wait for the water to evaporate as much as possible and the masala thickens and tada! Its ready for your big tummy!
